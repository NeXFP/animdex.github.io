#4 Anime Recommended by Nexus

Violet Evergarden

Genre: Coming-of-age

Directed by: Taichi Ishidate & Haruka Fujita

Studio: Kyoto Animation

Original Run: January 11, 2018 - April 5, 2018

Number of Episodes: 13


The State of Visuals

This show is regarded as one of the greatest visually strong anime to ever release. The art looks so surreal that you would think
that one is in the world themselves. From a sad backstory of the main character to moments of despair and desire to change, this anime boasts one of the best
OSTs to ever release and some of the best animation shown so far amongst anime that has released. Along with a strong female lead, I would say this is a strong
contender to fall under the category of a masterpiece.
