Comedy that breaks the 4th Wall

Gintama is mainly a comedy that focuses on breaking the 4th wall and referencing many different anime with either roasting the anime
or paying homage to specific moments in other shows. The show also does a great job on developing characters, I mean for an anime that has over 15 seasons
one would expect for good development. The show also has great story and animation during battle arcs and I would easily put this as one of the most underrated
anime to ever release that wasn't particularly mainstream.
