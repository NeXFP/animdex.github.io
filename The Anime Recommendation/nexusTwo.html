#2 Anime Recommended by Nexus

Run With The Wind (Kaze ga Tsyoku Futeiru)

Genre: Comedy, Sports, Drama

Directed by: Kazuya Nomura

Studio: Production I. G

Original Run: October 2, 2018 - March 26, 2019

Number of Episodes: 23


Sports turn strangers into friends

From a show starting off as something not many people have seen before, this show does an amazing job of developing each character into almost a main character.
At some point the show contains drama that fuels the drive of each individual person to do their best. I think this anime captures how people who actually played sports,
may feel when they've had these moments. This show was a masterpiece in my book, not only in animation or OSTs, but character building and story. I don't think I'll ever run into a sports anime
that will ever make me feel this emotional towards the success of characters ever again.
