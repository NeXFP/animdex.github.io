The Tissue Box

Let me just start of by saying, you need to watch Clannad prior to watching this show, but if you have, make sure to carry a box of tissues
next to you because it's going to be empty by the time you're done with this show. I don't think there is much I can explain as the art itself might not appeal
to some viewers who don't enjoy older art styles, but I recommend you go into this show with an open mind and honestly you will be left with yourself lost in your emotions.
